Heart Attack Prediction Model

A heart attack, also known as a myocardial infarction, occurs when a part of your heart muscle doesn't get enough blood. This damage can lead to lasting heart damage and even death. The most common symptom of a heart attack is chest pain or pressure. It may feel like squeezing, like a weight is on your chest, or like heartburn.

Several factors can increase your risk of a heart attack, including:

  • High blood pressure
  • High cholesterol
  • Smoking
  • Diabetes
  • Obesity
  • Family history of heart disease
